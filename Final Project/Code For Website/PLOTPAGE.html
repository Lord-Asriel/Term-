You awake in a strange chamber, with a countdown clock on the wall. A strange voice welcomes you to the bowels of Aperture Laboratories. The next thing you know, upon completon of the timer, a orange hole magically appears in the wall. You enter it, only to realise that this is a portal. A wormhole to traverse space and bypass time.

Portal is a puzzle game created by Valve in the year 2007. In this master piece the player must traverse "test chambers" by solving physics puzles using a "portal gun". Portal is the winner of 2008 game of the year my Develepers Choice Awards. The game received a sequel in 2011.


"Momentum, a function of mass and velocity, is conserved between portals. In layman's terms, speedy thing goes in, speedy thing comes out."


























You awake in a strange room, with a clock counting down. A strange mechanical voice starts to speak to you, indroducing you for the first time to the terrible Aperture Labarotories. In this emersive FPS, your only weapon is your genius, and your puzzle solving skills.

The "Aperture Handeled Portal Device" alows you to bend space by creating connecting portals to instantly traverse space. This puzzel game challenges the player to think before you act.

Will you be able to solve these demented "tests" layed in your way by some hidden AI, or will you perish in the attempt?
